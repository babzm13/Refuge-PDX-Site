Refuge Portland Information

Contact Us through Facebook

Our Facebook page is not super active, but people are frequently online! If you have questions and want answers, feel free to post up there, and we'll be happy to respond. The closed group status is to deter bots. Don't be deterred yourself!

Our Discord Channel

Our Discord Server is SUPER active and raring to go! We have events on there regularly, and it seems like someone is always around to answer questions and help solve problems. You are encouraged to start here, no matter what your needs and desires!

We use various social media platforms for our LARP. Our main area is Discord. It offers a very central location for chat, visual media, event planning and more. If there is one place you want to be, it is our Discord Server! Our plot teams are constantly running online content there, we have IG and OOG chat spaces, and lots of ways to get to know people before you take the plunge and join us in person! Announcements will let you know what is coming up and when, so you can stay up-to-date on the important stuff.

Facebook is another great tool for our your use. Announcements are cross-posted there, so you won't ever miss out, and people are frequently online and happy to answer questions. It's not as active as our Discord Server, but it's still a good place to hang out.

Interested in more? Our international organization has an Instagram and a Youtube Channel. Feel free to give those a follow, if you're interested!
