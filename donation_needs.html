Donation Needs

Needs for Refuge Portland

The following lists the needs and wants for Refuge Portland. It was last updated on 2/3/2022. If you have questions, please email logistics. If you are donating physical goods and the list hasn't been updated in the last 3 months, PLEASE EMAIL LOGISTICS BEFORE YOU DONATE to confirm we still need those things, otherwise, we may ask you to return the item and not give you Chapter Points (CP) for it.

Have something that's not on the list? You will need to first CONTACT PLOT OR THE OWNER to verify it's something we need and want. Once that is done, please submit a receipt (if you have one) to logistics, and CC the owner and the appropriate plot team. If you do not include the CC, your donation may not be processed.

Items that are red and/or bolded are urgent requests. We really need these before the next game, if possible! Thank you!
